Bio:
Anders Vestergaard is a composer and improviser and he plays the drums. Anders has toured worldwide and is a part of the bands Yes Deer, Baujillaud, Girls in Airports, Jesper Zeuthen / Jacob Anderskov / Anders Vestergaard, Blomsten and Laser Nun and has more than 40 releases on different labels. He also works as a solo artist and has released 2 solo albums (“eel” 2016 and “Prime Float / Unitary Perfect” 2019) with a third one on its way very soon. Besides his steady bands he has collaborated with artist such as Evan Parker, Michiyo Yagi, Lucas Abela, Marcela Lucatelli, Taku Unami and Toshimaru Nakamura, and has performed at festivals such as Sound of Stockholm, Roskilde Festival, Offene Ohren, AMR Festival, Mózg Festival, All Ears, COAX Festival etc. He also co-runs the label Abstract Tits with Aron Dahl.
